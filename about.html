About

A bit about my journey so far.

I hold a B.Tech in Computer Science and a Master of Arts in Music, and I have qualified the UGC NET exam too. My work is interdisciplinary, blending music with science (frequency-based studies), mathematics (taal divisions, permutations and combinations of notes), languages (composing literary texts), and even sports (explaining rhythms through basketball dribbles, etc.).

Beyond teaching, I actively train students, produce and record music, and manage live performances for shows and events – a role that keeps me learning every day. I am also passionate about research and have presented my work at national and international conferences.
